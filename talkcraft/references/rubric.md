# Review Rubric

## Scoring Model

Total score: `100`

### 1. Promise And Objective (`15`)

- clear audience
- clear objective
- explicit promise

### 2. Governing Idea (`10`)

- concise slogan
- single coherent thesis

### 3. Structure (`20`)

- `3-5` named fences
- each fence has one job
- sequence feels motivated
- each fence has evidence and a transition

### 4. Opening (`10`)

- stakes
- relevance
- orientation
- promise

### 5. Evidence And Concreteness (`15`)

- examples
- demos
- comparisons
- numbers or visuals where needed

### 6. Transitions (`10`)

- verbal punctuation
- explicit movement between fences

### 7. Close (`10`)

- strong recap
- implication or decision
- no weak fade-out

### 8. Rehearsal Readiness (`10`)

- time budget
- trim plan
- likely questions
- recovery move if time or demo slips

## Review Language

Use language like:

- `Missing promise`
- `Fence overloaded`
- `Abstract section`
- `Weak transition`
- `Close underpowered`
- `Timing risk`

Prefer direct, revision-friendly comments over praise-heavy prose.
